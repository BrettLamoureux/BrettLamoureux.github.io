My game is a Coloring Book game. In this game you will take control of the mouse and begin your coloring adventure through different environments. When you are ready click Start game! You will be taken to the instructions and environment select screen. When you choose a place, you will see numbers on all the shapes in the place you chose, on the bottom of the screen you will see little color squares with numbers on them as well. Click the colored square that you want to become the color you color with. Once clicked, when you click and drag your mouse you will have the color! Now your objective, fill in each shape with the corresponding color. Once finished look at your masterpiece and move on to a different environment! Happy Coloring!
